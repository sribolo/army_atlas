Privacy Policy

ARMY Atlas is an independent fan-made community project created for learning, creativity, and connecting ARMY around the world. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by BIGHIT MUSIC or HYBE.

Information we collect

To provide the app, we may store:

  • Your account information (username, email, and an encrypted password).
  • Profile information you choose to share, such as your bio, bias, social links, and profile photo.
  • Memories, comments, posts, and other content you create.
  • Photos and videos you upload (stored securely through Cloudinary).

How your information is used

Your information is only used to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Display your profile and content within the community.
  • Provide app features and improve the project.

What we don't do

We do not:

  • Sell your personal information.
  • Use your information for advertising.
  • Share your data with third parties except when required to operate the app (such as secure hosting or media storage).

Data retention

ARMY Atlas is a personal learning project. The service, database, or features may be updated, reset, or discontinued without notice.

Contact

If you have questions about your data or would like your account removed, please contact the project administrator.
